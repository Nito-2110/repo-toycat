FAQ

As we mentioned, we all have questions! But with our busy team responding to every single one would take eons, so feel free to browse here and check if your question has been answered already!

Or for more specific stuff, fill scroll all the way down to get in contact with us!

How long do shipments take?

We usually ship an item 2 - 3 weeks after the order has been placed.

After shipping, delivery usually takes about 2 weeks depending on your location, all of our items ship from the UK.

Do you ship internationally? / How can I track my order?

We ship to all countries available in DHL!

Whenever you place an order, a receipt with a tracking number will be sent to the e-mail you put your order to! Follow the instructions from DHL in your browser of preference!

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

We totally get it — sometimes you hit "buy" a little too fast! While we can’t guarantee changes after the order’s placed (our team moves quick!), shoot us an email as soon as possible and we’ll do our best to help before it ships out.

What is your return policy?

If something’s not quite right, no worries! We accept returns within 30 days of delivery — as long as the item is unworn, unwashed, and still in its original condition. Just reach out to our support team and they’ll guide you through the process.

My item arrived damaged — what should I do?

If your item showed up damaged, send us a photo and your order number — we’ll make it right ASAP.

What sizes are available?

Most items come in sizes XS through 3XL (sometimes even more)! You can check the product page for a size chart to find your perfect fit. If you’re between sizes, we usually suggest sizing up for that cozy feel.

Will the design fade or peel after washing?

Nope! As long as you follow the care instructions (like cold wash, inside out, low heat drying), your design should stay looking fresh for the long haul. We use high-quality printing made to last.
